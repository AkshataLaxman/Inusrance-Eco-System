About Insurance Clone

Insurance Clone is a modern, customer-focused insurance provider committed to making insurance simple, affordable, and hassle-free.

Founded in 2020, Insurance Clone has quickly grown to become one of India's leading digital insurance companies. We leverage technology to eliminate paperwork, reduce costs, and provide a seamless experience from quote to claim.

Our mission is to revolutionize the insurance industry by:

  • Simplifying the insurance buying process
  • Offering transparent pricing with no hidden charges
  • Providing instant policy issuance
  • Delivering hassle-free claims
  • Offering 24/7 customer support

We are backed by leading investors and are regulated by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI).
